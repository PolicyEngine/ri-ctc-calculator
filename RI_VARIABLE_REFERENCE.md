# Rhode Island PolicyEngine Variable Reference

## CTC Variables (created by reform)
- `ri_ctc_eligible_children` - Count of eligible children
- `ri_ctc_maximum` - Maximum CTC before phaseout
- `ri_ctc_phaseout` - Phaseout amount
- `ri_total_ctc` - **Total RI CTC** (main variable to use)
- `ri_refundable_ctc` - Refundable portion of CTC
- `ri_non_refundable_ctc` - Non-refundable portion of CTC

## Exemption Variables
- `ri_exemptions` - Total RI exemptions

## Income Variables
- `ri_agi` - RI Adjusted Gross Income
- `ri_taxable_income` - RI taxable income

## Tax Variables
- `ri_income_tax_before_non_refundable_credits` - Tax before non-refundable credits are applied
- `ri_income_tax_before_refundable_credits` - Tax before refundable credits are applied
- `ri_income_tax` - Final RI income tax after all credits
- `ri_non_refundable_credits` - Total non-refundable credits
- `ri_refundable_credits` - Total refundable credits

## Other Credit Variables
- `ri_eitc` - RI Earned Income Tax Credit
- `ri_cdcc` - RI Child and Dependent Care Credit
- `ri_property_tax_credit` - RI Property Tax Credit
- `ri_child_tax_rebate` - RI Child Tax Rebate

## Household Variables
- `household_net_income` - Total household net income
